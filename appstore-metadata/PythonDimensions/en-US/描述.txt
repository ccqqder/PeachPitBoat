"Points are vocabulary. Lines are syntax. Planes are logic. Construct your coding literacy in three dimensions."

Whether you are a liberal arts student starting from scratch, a university student preparing for TQC+, or a CS major aiming for CPE, Python Dimensions helps you build a solid programming foundation through systematic practice and absolute privacy protection.

Designed with a unique "Japanese Stationery" aesthetic, this app combines the warmth of traditional paper-and-ink design with cutting-edge On-Device AI and a built-in Python Compiler, allowing you to immerse yourself in coding anytime, anywhere—completely offline.

【Key Features】

3-Dimensional Training: Master Python Step-by-Step

Point (MCQ): Master fundamentals and vocabulary through multiple-choice questions.

Line (Cloze): Strengthen syntax structure by filling in key code segments.

Plane (Parsons): Train algorithmic logic by rearranging code blocks.

Your Personal On-Device AI Tutor

Powered by advanced Local LLMs and RAG technology.

The AI analyzes your mistakes to provide personalized hints and detailed explanations, guiding you to the solution without privacy risks.

100% Privacy & Offline: All chats and computations run locally. No data is ever sent to the cloud!

Write and run code instantly with the built-in Python 3.13 runtime.

Includes 10 practical code templates and AI-powered code generation to bridge the gap between theory and practice.

Smart Review & Analytics Dashboard

Automatically tracks incorrect answers and visualizes weaknesses using Radar Charts and Knowledge Heatmaps across 8 dimensions.

Tailored for Certification Exams

Over 300 curated questions covering PCEP, TQC+, and CPE certifications.

Boost exam prep with "Concept Cards" and a "Keyword Dictionary".
