Atomic Presence: Your Fortress of Digital Truth

In an age dominated by deepfakes and sophisticated video manipulation, how do you prove your recorded words are truly yours? Atomic Presence is a cutting-edge technical defense tool designed for interviewees, journalists, and legal professionals. It empowers you with "Technical Self-Verification" to safeguard the integrity of your media.

How It Works
Atomic Presence utilizes a multi-layered cryptographic approach to ensure that any attempt to edit, delete, or splice your media is mathematically detectable.

Cryptographic Hash Chain: The app generates a real-time SHA-256 hash sequence, updated at high frequency and encoded into dynamic QR codes. By capturing these codes in a recording, you create a continuous "digital heartbeat" that breaks if even a single frame is altered.

On-Device Digital Signatures: Using the Curve25519 algorithm via Apple’s CryptoKit, the app signs your session data locally. This creates non-repudiable proof of origin tied specifically to your device.

Audio Watermarking (FSK): In Witness Mode, the app embeds near-ultrasonic (18kHz/19kHz) frequency-shift keying signals into your audio. This forensic watermark persists even if the audio is separated from the video.

Built-in Forensic Verifier: Analyze external or internal recordings frame-by-frame. The app uses the Vision framework to extract QR codes, re-calculate the hash chain, and verify signatures to provide a detailed "Verified" or "Tampered" report.

Four Tiers of Protection
Level 0: Screensaver Mode
A low-pressure visual presence tool featuring a bouncing logo and starfield animation. Ideal for standby situations or simple time-stamping.

Level 1: Alert Mode (Psychological Defense)
Displays a real-time hash chain and dynamic QR code. Includes Picture-in-Picture (PiP) support, allowing you to keep the cryptographic chain visible while using other apps. Designed for external cameras to record your screen as proof of time and presence.

Level 2: Witness Mode (Audio Integrity)
Adds professional-grade audio recording (AAC or Lossless ALAC) with embedded FSK watermarking. Generates a signed .evidence.json file linking the audio checksum to the cryptographic session.

Level 3: Sovereign Mode (Full-Spectrum Defense)
The ultimate defense tool. Activates the front-facing camera with a real-time security HUD. While the video remains "clean" for maximum clarity, the app generates a localized digital signature and file checksum, ensuring the video file cannot be altered post-recording without breaking the signature.
