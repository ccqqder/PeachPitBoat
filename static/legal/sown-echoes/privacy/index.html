Last Updated / 最後更新日期: 2026-03-29

English

1. Overview

Sown Echoes (回聲種子, hereinafter referred to as "this App") is an iOS application developed by Chengche Lee that lets users record their thoughts, values, and life experiences as structured data for AI training and digital immortality. We adhere to a strict privacy-first policy.

In short: All core processing happens entirely on your device. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. No user data is collected.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Data We Do NOT Collect

This App does not collect:

  • Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as name, email, or phone number
  • Location data (location is user-entered text only, no GPS tracking)
  • Device Identifiers (IDFA, IDFV)
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Any form of user behavior tracking

Locally Stored Data

The following data is stored strictly on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework:

  • Text & Voice Entries: Your personal records, stored in the app's local database.
  • Questionnaire Responses: Answers to structured self-exploration questions.
  • Inscriptions: Personal motto, epitaph, and other deeply personal texts.
  • AI Analysis Results: Values radar, persona summaries, and word clouds, all generated on-device.
  • Digital Twin Conversations: Echo chat history, processed entirely on-device.
  • Cryptographic Identity: BIP-39 mnemonic phrase and Ed25519 private key, securely stored in iOS Keychain.

3. Cryptographic Identity

This App uses a cryptographic identity system instead of traditional accounts:

  • BIP-39 Mnemonic: A 12-word recovery phrase generated locally on your device.
  • Ed25519 Key Pair: Derived from the mnemonic using BIP-32 key derivation, stored in iOS Keychain.
  • Meme ID: A SHA-256 truncated public key hash used as your pseudonymous identifier.
  • No Account Required: No email, no password, no server-side registration.

Your mnemonic phrase is the only way to recover your identity. We cannot recover it for you. No user data is collected.

4. iCloud Sync

This App uses Apple iCloud for two purposes:

  • Private Database: Encrypted backup of your personal records. Only accessible by your Apple ID. Apple manages iCloud encryption and security.
  • Keychain Sync: Securely syncs your cryptographic identity to your other devices via iCloud Keychain.

The developer has no access to your iCloud data.

5. Public Contributions (Optional)

You may optionally choose to publish your writings to the Human Wisdom Library under a CC-BY-SA 4.0 license. When you do:

  • PII Stripping: Personal names, phone numbers, emails, national ID numbers, credit card numbers, and addresses are automatically removed before publication.
  • Date Coarsening: Timestamps are reduced to month-level granularity.
  • PII Audit: A defense-in-depth audit scans stripped content for any residual personal information.
  • No GPS: No precise geographic location is ever recorded or published.
  • Withdrawal: If you have established an identity, you can withdraw your public contributions at any time.
  • Quality Gate: On-device AI validates content quality before publication.

Public contributions are stored in Apple's CloudKit Public Database and may be used for AI model training. You can browse the published dataset at huggingface.co/datasets/qqder/memelives-open-contributions.

6. On-Device AI Processing

All AI features operate on-device with a three-tier fallback system:

  • Apple Foundation Models (iOS 26+): Apple's built-in on-device models.
  • Local LLM (MLX): Optional downloadable models (Qwen 0.5B–3B) stored in App Group container.
  • Stub Fallback: Basic functionality without AI when no model is available.

AI features include: speech-to-text (Whisper), values analysis, persona summarization, digital twin chat (Echo), and content quality assessment.

No AI processing is performed on external servers. No user data is collected.

7. Device Permissions

This App requires the following device permissions, all used exclusively for local functionality:

  • Microphone: Used to record voice entries for on-device Whisper transcription. Audio is stored only on your device and never uploaded.
  • Speech Recognition: Used for on-device voice-to-text conversion. All processing happens locally.

8. Third-Party Services

This App does NOT use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking frameworks. No Google Analytics, No Facebook SDK, No Firebase, No Ads, No Crash Reporting services.

9. Data Export

You can export your data in multiple formats (SFT JSONL, Digital Twin JSON, Personal Backup). All exports are generated on-device and saved to your local Files. PII is automatically stripped from training-format exports.

10. Children's Privacy

This App is not designed for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children. Public contributions require users to confirm they are at least 16 years of age.

  • Right to Withdraw Consent (Art. 7): Withdraw public contributions at any time. Note: contributions already included in published HuggingFace dataset releases remain under CC-BY-SA 4.0 license.

Data Retention

Local data is retained until you delete it. Public contributions are retained in CloudKit Public Database until you withdraw them. Withdrawn contributions are permanently deleted from CloudKit. Published dataset snapshots on HuggingFace are versioned and immutable.

In the event of a personal data breach affecting public contributions, the data controller will notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, where feasible and where notification is required under Art. 33 GDPR. If the breach is likely to result in a high risk to the rights and freedoms of data subjects, affected users will be notified without undue delay via the app's support email and/or an in-app notification. To report a suspected breach or for breach-related inquiries, please contact: qqder339@gmail.com.

International Data Transfers

Public contributions are stored in Apple's CloudKit infrastructure. Apple complies with GDPR data transfer requirements under its own Data Processing Addendum. The HuggingFace dataset is hosted in the EU (France). No additional cross-border transfers are performed by the developer.

Pseudonymous Data

Public contributions are pseudonymous: your Meme ID (a cryptographic hash) links your contributions but cannot be traced to your real identity without possession of your BIP-39 mnemonic phrase. Under GDPR, pseudonymous data remains personal data.

Right to Lodge a Complaint

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority in the EU Member State of your habitual residence, place of work, or place of the alleged infringement.

繁體中文

1. 概述

Sown Echoes（回聲種子，以下簡稱「本應用」）是一款由 Chengche Lee 開發的 iOS 應用程式，讓使用者能夠記錄個人思想、價值觀和生命經歷，作為 AI 訓練與數位永生的結構化資料。我們奉行嚴格的隱私優先政策。

簡單來說：所有核心處理皆在您的裝置上完成。我們不會收集、儲存或傳輸任何個人資料至外部伺服器。不收集任何使用者資料。

2. 資料收集與使用

我們不收集的資料

本應用不會收集以下資料：

  • 個人身份資訊（姓名、電子郵件、電話號碼等）
  • 位置資訊（位置為使用者自行輸入的文字，不使用 GPS 追蹤）
  • 裝置識別碼（IDFA、IDFV）
  • 使用行為分析資料
  • 任何形式的追蹤資料

本地儲存的資料

以下資料僅使用 Apple SwiftData 框架儲存在您的裝置上：

  • 文字與語音記錄： 您的個人記錄，儲存於應用程式本地資料庫。
  • 問卷回答： 結構化自我探索問題的回答。
  • 銘文： 座右銘、墓誌銘等深度個人文字。
  • AI 分析結果： 價值觀雷達、人格摘要和文字雲，全部在裝置上產生。
  • 數位分身對話： Echo 聊天記錄，完全在裝置上處理。
  • 密碼學身份： BIP-39 助記詞和 Ed25519 私鑰，安全儲存於 iOS 鑰匙圈。

3. 密碼學身份

本應用使用密碼學身份系統取代傳統帳號：

  • BIP-39 助記詞： 在您的裝置上本地產生的 12 字復原短語。
  • Ed25519 金鑰對： 使用 BIP-32 金鑰衍生從助記詞產生，儲存於 iOS 鑰匙圈。
  • Meme ID： SHA-256 截斷的公鑰雜湊值，作為您的假名識別碼。
  • 不需要帳號： 不需要電子郵件、密碼或伺服器端註冊。

您的助記詞是復原身份的唯一方式。我們無法為您復原。不收集任何使用者資料。

4. iCloud 同步

本應用使用 Apple iCloud 進行兩項用途：

  • 私人資料庫： 加密備份您的個人記錄。僅您的 Apple ID 可存取。Apple 管理 iCloud 的加密和安全性。
  • 鑰匙圈同步： 透過 iCloud 鑰匙圈安全同步您的密碼學身份至您的其他裝置。

開發者無法存取您的 iCloud 資料。

5. 公開貢獻（選擇性）

您可以選擇將作品以 CC-BY-SA 4.0 授權發布至 Human Wisdom Library。當您這麼做時：

  • PII 移除： 個人姓名、電話號碼、電子郵件、身分證號碼、信用卡號碼和地址會在發布前自動移除。
  • 時間粗化： 時間戳記降低至月份粒度。
  • PII 審計： 深度防禦審計掃描已清除的內容，檢查是否有殘留的個人資訊。
  • 無 GPS： 不會記錄或發布任何精確地理位置。
  • 撤回： 如果您已建立身份，可以隨時撤回您的公開貢獻。
  • 品質閘門： 裝置上的 AI 在發布前驗證內容品質。

公開貢獻儲存於 Apple CloudKit 公開資料庫，可能被用於 AI 模型訓練。您可以在 huggingface.co/datasets/qqder/memelives-open-contributions 瀏覽已發布的資料集。

6. 裝置端 AI 處理

所有 AI 功能使用三層降級系統在裝置上運行：

  • Apple Foundation Models（iOS 26+）：Apple 內建的裝置端模型。
  • 本機 LLM（MLX）：可選下載的模型（Qwen 0.5B–3B），儲存於 App Group 容器。
  • Stub 後備： 無可用模型時的基本功能。

AI 功能包括：語音轉文字（Whisper）、價值觀分析、人格摘要、數位分身對話（Echo）及內容品質評估。

所有 AI 處理皆不在外部伺服器上執行。不收集任何使用者資料。

7. 裝置權限

本應用需要以下裝置權限，所有權限僅用於本地功能：

  • 麥克風： 用於錄製語音記錄，進行裝置端 Whisper 轉錄。音訊僅儲存在您的裝置上，不會上傳。
  • 語音辨識： 用於裝置端語音轉文字。所有處理在本地完成。

8. 第三方服務

本應用 不使用 任何第三方分析、廣告或追蹤框架。無 Google Analytics、無 Facebook SDK、無 Firebase、無廣告、無崩潰報告服務。

9. 資料匯出

您可以將資料匯出為多種格式（SFT JSONL、Digital Twin JSON、個人備份）。所有匯出在裝置上產生並儲存至本地檔案。訓練格式匯出會自動移除 PII。

10. 兒童隱私

本應用不針對 13 歲以下兒童設計，也不會故意收集兒童的個人資訊。公開貢獻要求使用者確認年滿 16 歲。
